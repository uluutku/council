# Product

## Proposition

Council is a private web messenger where people can maintain direct conversations with real
people, built-in AI experts, built-in AI personas, and private custom AI characters. Human and AI
contacts share an inbox but are always visibly distinguished.

The core promise is private messaging and persistent AI contacts in one application.

## Target users

Council is intended for people who want ordinary direct messaging and durable AI relationships or
workflows without moving between unrelated applications. It serves users who value explicit
memory controls, private custom personas, persistent work artifacts, and visibility into provider
use and cost.

## Messaging scope

The first release supports human-to-human direct conversations and human-to-AI direct
conversations. It is a responsive web application and may be installable as a PWA where
practical. A native Flutter application is planned later.

Human group chats, AI participants inside human conversations, AI-to-AI conversations, public
channels, and a social feed are not in the first release.

## AI contacts

Persona contacts are relationship-oriented roles such as companions, Socratic mentors,
accountability partners, language-practice partners, and private custom fictional or friend
personas.

Expert contacts are task-oriented roles: Researcher, Planner, Coding Assistant, Writing Editor,
and Fact Checker. Medical diagnosis, legal-decision, and financial-adviser agents are not launch
roles.

Custom personas remain private and cannot initially be published or shared.

## Access model

Free accounts retain full human direct messaging and receive a limited, credit-based AI trial.
The trial begins with the first AI message and expires after seven days or credit exhaustion.
Exact allowances are configuration.

Pro accounts receive a monthly AI credit balance and unlock built-in AI contacts, custom
personas, memory modes, image understanding, tools, artifacts, and controlled forwarding. Council
does not promise unlimited AI usage. AI pauses when credits are exhausted; human messaging
continues.

## Explicitly postponed

- Human groups and public channels
- AI agents joining human conversations
- Native mobile applications
- Voice/video calls and voice cloning
- Public persona marketplace and shared custom personas
- Local AI inference and AI-to-AI conversations
- Autonomous background agents
- Medical diagnosis and legal-decision agents
- Unlimited AI plans
- End-to-end encryption
- Social feed
